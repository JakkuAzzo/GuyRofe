About

I'm an emerging photographer with a BA (Hons) in Photography, experienced in studio, event and location work – from graduation ceremonies at the University of Wolverhampton to the National Film Awards, National Reality TV Awards and architecture clients such as 121 Collective.

My work centres on true-to-life, meaningful storytelling through photography. I'm comfortable setting up and running a studio alone, directing nervous clients, and working under time pressure while keeping image quality consistent. Over the next few years, my goal is to build a sustainable career in a creative field, progressing from assistant or junior roles towards full-time freelance photography.
