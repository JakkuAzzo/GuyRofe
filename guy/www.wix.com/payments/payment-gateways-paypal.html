FAQ: 

Which PayPal payment options are available to my customers?

Wix offers PayPal Checkout, Pay in 4**, Pay Monthly*** and Venmo. Customers will see the most relevant PayPal payment options when they shop your website.

What’s the difference between Pay in 4 and Pay Monthly?

Pay in 4 is a short-term installment option that lets U.S.-based customers make 4 interest-free, bi-weekly payments, with the first payment due at the time of purchase, on purchases from $30 to $1,500.


Pay Monthly is a payment option for larger purchases from $199 to $10,000. It lets U.S.– based customers pay in 6, 12 or 24 monthly installments. Pay Monthly gives customers predictable monthly payments at varying APRs. See terms.

What is Venmo?

Venmo is a convenient payment method which lets customers share, like, and comment on purchases. It is available for users based in the US who have connected their sites to PayPal. Once you’re connected to PayPal, the Venmo button will appear at your checkout as a payment option—so your customers can pay with Venmo right away.

How can I offer PayPal as a payment option for customers?

Wix now offers two ways to connect PayPal to your site–Wix Payments, or externally.
By connecting PayPal with Wix Payments, a new PayPal account will be created for you - allowing you to then manage all sales (PayPal included) right in your Wix Dashboard.
If you prefer you can still externally connect an existing PayPal account.

Can I use PayPal with other payment methods?

Yes, you can add multiple payment methods that work with PayPal, like credit card payments through Wix Payments, digital wallets and more.

Which currencies are supported by PayPal?

Check out PayPal’s supported currencies list.

What are the processing fees?

PayPal charges a fixed processing fee for all 3 payment options. Also, you don’t pay any additional fees for using a third-party payment gateway on Wix.

How do I add PayPal buttons to my online store?

If you’re already connected to PayPal, the buttons will appear in your cart and checkout automatically. You can enable them on your product pages as well.

If you haven’t added PayPal to your site yet or don’t have a PayPal account, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your site dashboard > Settings

  2. Click Accept Payments

  3. Next to PayPal, select Connect and follow the instructions
