7.

Where can I view my payments?

No matter which payment provider you choose, you get a full overview of your payments in your Wix dashboard—online or in the Wix app.

8.

What types of businesses are supported on Wix?

This depends. There’s a difference between what you can sell on Wix vs. what you can sell with Wix Payments, our own provider. Wix Payments supports many business types, but certain products and services are prohibited, which is why you must review the Wix Payments Prohibited Products and Services list. If your business type is on the list, you might still be able to sell on Wix with one of the payment gateways we offer (as long as it’s legal and you comply with our Terms of Use, of course). In any case, before you get started, check with your chosen provider to learn more about their prohibited products and services.

FAQ

1.

Which payment providers are offered on Wix?

We offer over 80+ leading payment providers for you to choose from, including Wix Payments, PayPal, Stripe and Square. View our full list of payment providers for more info.

Learn how to choose the best payment provider for your business here.

2.

Which online payment gateways are available in my country?

Once you create your Wix account, our platform automatically selects a payment gateway for your region—you’ll see this in your Wix dashboard.

Check out all available online payment gateways by country here.

3.

What payment methods are supported by Wix?

To accept debit/credit card payments, choose one payment provider for your business. However, we recommend adding multiple payment methods, so your customers have a variety of ways to pay at checkout. You can add digital wallets like PayPal, buy now, pay later solutions, bank transfers and more. Note that different payment methods vary by location and payment provider.

4.

How do I connect an online payment gateway on Wix?

  • Go to your Wix dashboard > Accept Payments

  • Choose your preferred online payment gateway

  • Follow their instructions to start accepting payments

For more information, check out this article.

5.

I chose a payment provider but changed my mind. Can I switch to a different one?

Sure, you can change your provider anytime. Learn how to do this here.

6.

Will I need to pay extra fees for using third-party payment gateways on Wix?

We don’t charge additional transaction fees for using third-party payment gateways on our platform. Generally, your processing fees are determined by the provider—most charge a payment processing fee based on your payment method and transaction values. For example, Wix Payments, our native provider, charges one fixed rate for all credit cards: a percentage of your transaction value, plus a fixed amount.
