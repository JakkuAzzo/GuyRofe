Drag & drop website builder FAQ

What is a drag & drop website builder?

A drag & drop website builder is a user-friendly tool that lets you design and customize your website by dragging and dropping elements onto your page. It simplifies the website creation, so you don't need coding skills.

What is the best drag & drop website builder?

Wix is considered the best drag & drop website builder, offering flexibility, user-friendliness, and a wide range of features to bring your vision to life seamlessly.

What are the advantages of a drag & drop website builder?

The advantages of a drag & drop website builder include easy website design, time efficiency, and complete control over your website's elements. It's an ideal solution for users without coding expertise.
