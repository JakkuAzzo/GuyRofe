FAQ

What is Wix Studio? 

Wix Studio is a web creation platform built for all creators—from freelancers to agencies—that enables them to deliver exceptional websites with maximum efficiency. Marketing Suite is underpinned by Studio’s powerful yet intuitive design tools, Editor and infrastructure with a workspace that’s designed to flex with marketing teams at mid to large-scale organizations. And, Marketing Suite’s AI-powered creation capabilities have been designed to let marketers create campaigns at speed without compromising on quality. 

What’s the onboarding process like for Marketing Suite? 

Onboarding with Marketing Suite is straightforward. It’s an intuitive platform that you and your team can pretty much start using right away. Customers have a dedicated customer success manager who provides hands-on guidance throughout the entire process.

Can I integrate my existing tools and systems with Marketing Suite? 

Yes, Marketing Suite integrates seamlessly with tools like GA4, Figma, HubSpot, Salesforce and ActiveCampaign. Teams can also connect Marketing Suite with DAM (Digital Asset Management) and ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems via 100s of open APIs. 

Can I edit assets that have been generated with AI? 

Of course. Edit content, swap out images or incorporate last minute feedback seamlessly with our low-code editing and design tools. Once your assets are created, open up the Editor and make changes. 

What kind of support is available?

Customers have access to our 24/7 support team, as well as a dedicated success manager who provides personalized guidance to you and your team.

I’m already a Wix Studio user. Can I use Marketing Suite? How does the Marketing Suite integrate with my existing Wix Studio account? 

Yes, you can. If you’re an existing Wix Studio or Wix Studio for enterprise user, Marketing Suite will live in a separate workspace. Use the same login credentials for both workspaces and easily switch between them.  See here for more information on workspaces.
