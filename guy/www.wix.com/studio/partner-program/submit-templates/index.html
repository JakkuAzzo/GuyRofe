How it works

Read the full guidelines

STEP 1

Become a Partner in order to sell templates in the Marketplace. Partners also get exclusive benefits like priority support and revenue share.

STEP 2

Create a custom template on Wix Studio and submit it via the form. Make sure the template is aligned with our guidelines for the best chance of it being accepted.

STEP 3

Our team will review the template according to our standards and guidelines. We aim to review submissions within 60 business days, and we’ll keep you updated on the review process.

STEP 4

Once approved, your template will go live in the Marketplace so you can start selling.

What we’re looking for

Our team reviews each template submitted to check for quality and usability according to these guidelines:

Read the full guidelines

Design and content

The template is well designed, follows UX/UI best practices and is on-trend for its industry.  The content is not copyrighted and tells the story of the business type it’s for.

Functionality

If a template offers advanced functionality through one of our native business solutions or custom code, we’ll accept either a designed one that meets our guidelines, or a wireframe that’s well-structured and usable, similar to our Studio wireframes.

Layout and structure

Studio’s layouting tools are used properly to create pleasing compositions, with content presented nicely across breakpoints.

Exclusivity and uniqueness

The template doesn’t contain external marketing material and is significantly different from existing Studio templates or other templates in the Marketplace.

FAQs

Who can submit Wix Studio templates to sell in the Marketplace?

Any Wix Studio account that is part of the Partner program is eligible to submit templates to be sold in the Marketplace. 

How does the review process work?

All templates submitted are reviewed by our team according to these guidelines. The process can take up to 60 business days.

When will my template be listed in the Marketplace?

Once your template is reviewed and approved, we’ll notify you and it will be uploaded to the Marketplace, so you can start selling.

What should I do if a template I submit is not accepted?

We recommend taking a detailed look at our submissions guidelines. These resources can help you understand how to improve the template to meet our guidelines. We would also recommend looking at our example templates. Once the template meets our guidelines, you’re welcome to submit it again. 

How will I get paid when someone purchases one of my templates?

You’ll be paid as part of your revenue share earnings. Earnings from client sales, plans and templates are invoiced together. To qualify for a payout, your total earnings need to reach a minimum of $200. Once you meet this threshold, the payout cycle is net 60 days. Payouts appear in your bank account 3-6 days after we initiate the payment. You can find the full details about payouts in this article.

Will Wix take fees for each sale? 

In 2024 we won’t charge any fees—100% of the sale goes to you. 

Can I sell or promote a template I’ve submitted in other marketplaces?

Yes, you’re free to sell your template anywhere on the web—whether that’s in other marketplaces or on your own site. Soon, you’ll be able to download it as a shareable zip file and add it as a digital product anywhere you want.
