Our mission 

Wix Studio makes high-quality web creation intuitive and efficient for agencies and freelancers who build websites for clients. By taking the complexity out of the process, we help designers, developers and marketers in 190+ countries get more from their time, talent and teams. Wix Studio delivers the tools and workflows to produce your best work and keep projects moving. It’s a balance you won’t find anywhere else.

Why Wix Studio?

  • Wix Studio’s visual editor puts creative control in your hands, blending precision with flexibility. Built for design logic, it lets you align, layer and manage site-wide styles intuitively. The platform includes custom CSS options and seamless Figma integration—so every workflow feels effortless.

    Responsive by design
    Set custom breakpoints, scale layouts across devices, and ensure your sites look polished everywhere.

    No code animations
    Bring pages to life with motion effects, parallax, and more—add interactivity visually, without writing a single line of code.

    Reusable templates
    Speed up your process by saving, customizing and reusing templates, widgets and styled sections across projects.

  • Wix Studio streamlines every step of your web creation process—so agencies, studios and freelancers can focus on delivering exceptional results for clients. Manage multiple projects, collaborate in real time and hand off work smoothly, all from a single workspace.

    Unified team collaboration
    Keep designers, developers and marketers aligned with concurrent editing, on-canvas commenting and role-based permissions—so everyone can work together, without stepping on toes.

    Smooth client handoff
    Simplify post-launch with personalized client kits, automatic reports and Content Mode, making it easy for clients to update content while your design stays locked in.

    Built-in no-code CMS
    Create flexible, custom content structures and connect dynamic data—all visually and without code. Let clients manage their content independently, with zero risk to your site design.

  • Wix Studio adapts to any business need with full design control and flexibility. Customize every touchpoint, manage payments globally and leverage dedicated features for every industry.


    eCommerce
    Design every detail of online stores, product pages, and checkouts. Accept payments via 100+ providers and manage sales with built-in POS.

    Bookings
    Build service-based sites with seamless online scheduling and appointment management.

    Events & hospitality
    Run events, ticketing, RSVPs, menus, reservations and hospitality features—all from one platform.

    Blogs
    Share expertise and boost SEO with built-in blog tools designed for content-rich sites.
